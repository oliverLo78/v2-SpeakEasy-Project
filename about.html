About Speakeasy

Crafting the perfect cocktail experience

Speakeasy is a modern cocktail discovery platform designed to help both novice and experienced mixologists explore the world of cocktails. Our mission is to provide comprehensive information about thousands of drinks, complete with recipes, ingredients, and video tutorials.


Whether you're looking to master classic cocktails or discover new creations, Speakeasy offers an intuitive interface to search, save, and share your favorite drink recipes. Our platform combines data from TheCocktailDB API with video content from YouTube to create a rich, immersive experience for cocktail enthusiasts.
