    Top Shelf Recommendations

    Old Fashioned

    The old fashioned is a cocktail made by muddling sugar with bitters and water, adding whiskey, and garnishing with an orange slice or zest and a cocktail cherry.

    Cosmopolitan

    A cosmopolitan is a cocktail made with vodka, Cointreau, cranberry juice, and lime juice. Perfect for those who enjoy a balance of sweet and tart flavors.

    Mojito

    Mojito is a traditional Cuban punch. The cocktail often consists of five ingredients: white rum, sugar, lime juice, soda water, and mint.
